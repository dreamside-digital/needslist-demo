How it works

Over 70% of aid during emergencies goes to logistics. NeedsList disrupts the model by aggregating needs from the ground up by getting the right supplies to the right people at the right time.

1

NGOs post needs

Vetted local NGOs post their most urgent needs on NeedsList.

2

Search needs in real time

Users meet needs by purchasing supplies, volunteering, or donating.

3

Need met!

Supplies are delivered to the NGO from local suppliers whenever possible for distribution.
