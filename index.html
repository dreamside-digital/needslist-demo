How it works

In the traditional humanitarian aid system, huge amounts of resources go into logistics and operations. NeedsList provides aid more efficiently by leveraging technology to deliver resources directly to NGOs on the ground.

1

Vetted local NGOs post their most urgent needs on NeedsList.

2

Users search for and meet needs by purchasing supplies, volunteering, or donating.

3

Supplies are purchased from local suppliers whenever possible and delivered to the NGO for distribution.

Partners

NeedsList relies on a strong and resilient network of partners including private sector sponsors, trusted vendors, local vetted NGOs, and vetting partners.
